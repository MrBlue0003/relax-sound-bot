Post 2-3 per week max (subscribers want sounds, not noise in their feed).

## 📋 Posting strategy

**Tone rule:** soft, relatable, never salesy. Audience wants quiet — match their energy.

**Cadence:** 2 posts/week max. Sleep audience hates feed clutter.

**Best time to post:** Sunday evening (8-10 PM your time) and Wednesday evening — when people plan or recover from the week.

**Engage with comments** within 6 hours. Reply with sound recommendations like a friend, not a brand.
